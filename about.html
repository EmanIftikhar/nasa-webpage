Team Titan Voyagers


Titan Voyagers consists of six 3rd year Computer Science students studying in Heriot-Watt University Dubai. A group of close friends as well, this team bring the right balance of chemistry and skills in the aspect of game making. From creativity to technical implementation, this team has got it all. The group aims and aspires to reach a wide audience to inspire and instill values of education, space exploration and discovery.
